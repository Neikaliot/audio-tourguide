Hoàn Kiếm District: Where Tradition Meets Modern Hanoi

At the heart of Hanoi, Hoàn Kiếm district is a blend of history, culture, and the city’s vibrant urban life. From historic landmarks to bustling streets, this district embodies the essence of Hanoi.

Hoàn Kiếm Lake

Start your journey at Hoàn Kiếm Lake, a tranquil spot steeped in legend. According to local lore, Emperor Lê Lợi returned a magical sword to a golden turtle in this lake, giving it the name ‘Lake of the Returned Sword.’ The picturesque walking paths around the lake are perfect for a morning or evening stroll, and the iconic Turtle Tower in the middle of the lake adds a mystical touch.

Ngọc Sơn Temple

On the northern side of the lake, cross the striking red Húc Bridge to reach Ngọc Sơn Temple, dedicated to national hero Trần Hưng Đạo. The temple’s serene atmosphere and beautiful views make it a peaceful place to relax and take in the surroundings.

Old Quarter (Phố Cổ)

Next, lose yourself in the charm of Hanoi’s Old Quarter. Famous for its 36 streets, each named after traditional trades like silver, silk, or herbs, the Old Quarter is a maze of narrow, bustling streets where history and modern life coexist. Explore the local shops, cafes, and street food stalls that give this area its lively atmosphere.
